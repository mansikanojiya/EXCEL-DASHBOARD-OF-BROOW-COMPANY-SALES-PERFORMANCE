# EXCEL-DASHBOARD-OF-BROOW-COMPANY-SALES-PERFORMANCE

Sales Performance Dashboard (FY 2021–FY 2024) – Explanation
This Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across multiple years, regions, customers, and product categories. It is designed to help management quickly understand trends, profitability, and key business drivers.
1. Objective of the Dashboard
The main objective of this dashboard is to:
Track sales and profit performance from FY 2021 to FY 2024
Analyze category-wise, state-wise, and monthly trends
Identify top-performing customers and regions
Support data-driven decision-making
2. Key KPIs (Top Section)
At the top of the dashboard, we have two key performance indicators:
Total Sales: ₹19,28,687.88
This represents the overall revenue generated during the selected period.
Total Profit: ₹2,47,961.07
This shows the total profit earned, helping assess business profitability.
These KPIs give a quick snapshot of business performance.
3. Filters / Slicers
The dashboard includes interactive slicers for:
Year (2021–2024)
State (Alabama, Arizona, Arkansas, etc.)
These filters allow users to:
Drill down into specific years or states
Perform comparative analysis
View dynamic updates across all charts
4. Category-wise Sales
This horizontal bar chart shows sales distribution across product categories, such as:
Technology
Office Supplies
Furniture
It helps identify:
Top-performing categories
Categories that may require improvement or strategic focus
5. Monthly Sales Trend
The line chart displays sales by month (January–December).
Key insights from this chart:
Identifies seasonal patterns
Highlights peak and low sales months
Helps in forecasting and inventory planning
6. Quantity Sold by Month
This bar chart represents the total quantity sold each month.
It helps understand:
Customer purchasing behavior
Demand fluctuations across the year
Alignment between sales value and volume
7. Top 5 Profit Customers
This chart highlights the top customers contributing the most profit.
Business value:
Helps identify high-value customers
Supports customer retention strategies
Useful for targeted marketing and loyalty programs
8. Customer Count by Year
This visualization shows the number of customers per year.
Insights gained:
Growth or decline in customer base
Effectiveness of customer acquisition strategies
Overall business expansion trends
9. State-wise Sales (Map Visualization)
The map shows sales distribution across different states.
It helps:
Identify high-performing and low-performing regions
Support regional sales planning
Optimize resource allocation
10. Profit Year by Year
This bar chart compares profit across different financial years.
Key takeaways:
Tracks year-on-year profit growth
Highlights years with strong or weak performance
Assists in long-term strategic planning
